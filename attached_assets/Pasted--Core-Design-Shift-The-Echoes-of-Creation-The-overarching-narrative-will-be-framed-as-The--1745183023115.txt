**I. Game Mechanics – “Echo Resonance”**

*   **Core Mechanic:** “Echo Resonance” – Player actions generate
“Resonance” which interacts with the environment and the echoes. High
Resonance can trigger events, reveal hidden truths, or attract
dangerous echoes.
*   **Resonance Types:** (Derived from player actions)
    *   **Faith:** (Prayer, rituals, devotion to the “holy”
principles) – Increases visibility of benevolent echoes.
    *   **Doubt:** (Questioning, skepticism, confronting the truth) –
Attracts corrupted echoes.
    *   **Curiosity:** (Exploration, investigation, seeking knowledge)
– Creates unstable resonance, revealing echoes and altering the
environment.
*   **Echo Interaction:**
    *   **Distortion:** High Resonance causes distortions in the
environment – visual and auditory.
    *   **Manifestation:** Specific Resonance levels trigger echoes to
manifest, ranging from minor visual anomalies to full-blown
encounters.
    *   **Corruption:**  Excessive Doubt or Curiosity leads
to the ‘corruption’ of structures and the environment.
*   **Player Progression:** Focuses on learning to *manage* Resonance,
not traditional leveling. Abilities could include: “Resonance Shield”
(limited resistance to distortion), “Echo Sight” (briefly perceive
echoes), and “Resonance Manipulation” (carefully guide resonance –
high risk/reward).

**II. World Draft – “The Shattered Chord”**

*   **Setting:** The remnants of a once-great civilization, now known
as "The Shattered Chord." The Chord worshipped the 'Prime Harmony’ - a
fundamental principle believed to govern all of existence. The Chord
were masters of manipulating Resonance, but their hubris led to a
catastrophic event - the “Great Dissonance” – which shattered the
Prime Harmony and unleashed the echoes.
*   **Geography:**
    *   **The Obsidian Coast:** A jagged coastline dominated by
volcanic formations and shattered cities constructed from obsidian.
The area is highly unstable and riddled with corrupted Resonance.
    *   **The Echoing Forests:** Dense, ancient forests where the
boundaries between realities are thin. The trees themselves seem to
resonate with the echoes.
    *   **The Silent Peaks:**  Mountains that have been completely
drained of Resonance, creating zones of utter silence and dread.
*   **Key Locations:**
    *   **The Citadel of Silence:** The central location of The
Chord's worship. Now a decaying monolith, heavily corrupted, and the
epicenter of the Great Dissonance.
    *   **The Chronarium:** A partially intact observatory where The
Chord attempted to predict and control Resonance.
    *   **The Whispering Cairns:** Ancient stone structures used for
rituals – now haunted by lingering echoes.
*   **Factions:**
    *   **The Keepers of the Chord:** A dwindling order dedicated to
preserving the remnants of The Chord’s knowledge and attempting to
restore the Prime Harmony – misguided and increasingly fanatical.

danger and wisdom.
    *   **The Corrupted:** Those consumed entirely by the echoes, now
driven by primal instinct and a desire to spread the Dissonance.

**III. Core Narrative Hook:**

The player character is a ‘Resonator’ - an individual uniquely
